About the shader

Minecraft — the Fantasy way

Fantasy Shaders Reimagined is a shader pack built on top of Complementary Shaders. The goal is simple: make Minecraft more colorful, more striking, and genuinely vibrant, with a fully committed fantasy aesthetic.

Concretely, the shader reworks the overall game atmosphere: blued-out nights, brighter daytime, starry skies, visible auroras and nebulae, stronger reflections on water and surfaces, and the End dimension recolored in a deep violet inspired by the Endermen. Light rays gain in intensity, producing dramatic scenes at sunrise and sunset.

Core Features

What Fantasy Shaders changes

🌌
Blue Nights & Living Sky

Nights bathed in a deep fantasy blue. The sky is no longer flat — stars, auroras, clouds and nebulae appear above your builds. Night becomes a backdrop in its own right.

Detailed Characteristics

Everything that changes

🌙 Blue Nights & Animated Sky Vault
The night adopts a fantasy blue tone. The sky vault is no longer static: stars, auroras, clouds and nebulae animate above your constructions. The result is a night that isn't just dark — it becomes a full visual environment, clear and pleasant enough for exploration or building.
☀️ Brighter, Crisper Days
In full daylight, the horizon whitens and the sky vault shifts toward a clean light blue. Clouds follow the same logic, which reinforces volumes and depth without distorting the cube-based silhouette of the game. Colors are more vivid, shapes better defined.
🌄 More Colorful Sunrises & Sunsets
The shader pushes warm gradients further. Morning and evening transitions are more pronounced, with hues leaning toward gold and rose — saturated enough to be striking, but never enough to impair visibility. Silhouettes of builds and terrain are dramatically highlighted.
✨ Reflections & Volumetric Light
Surface reflections gain presence, especially at night where water can behave like a mirror. Volumetric light (rays through vegetation, openings, stained glass) is intensified — ideal for emphasizing the architecture of a base or city. Adds real depth to any structure.
🟣 End Dimension — Violet Overhaul
The End is entirely recolored around a violet palette consistent with the Endermen. The atmosphere becomes more coherent and readable, while the lighting better highlights shapes and platforms. It remains immediately recognizable as the End, but now feels intentional and immersive.
Installation Guide

Set up in 3 steps

01

Install Fabric Loader

Fantasy Shaders Reimagined is compatible with Fabric only. Download and install Fabric Loader for your Minecraft version (1.20+) from the official Fabric website. Run the installer and launch the game once to generate the mods/ folder.

FABRIC LOADER IS REQUIRED — Forge is NOT supported
02

Download the shader .jar

Click the download button below to get Fantasy_Shaders_Reimagined-2.5.5.jar from the official distribution. Save the file anywhere on your computer — you'll place it in the next step.

03

Place it in your mods folder

Move Fantasy_Shaders_Reimagined-2.5.5.jar into your Minecraft mods/ folder.
Windows: %appdata%\.minecraft\mods\
Mac: ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/mods/
Launch the game with the Fabric profile and enjoy the shader!
